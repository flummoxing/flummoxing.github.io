Who Are We?

My name is Fritz Frankfurt. I'm passionate about breakfast, but my journey to this point has been a complex one. When I was 23 or so, I experienced an existential crisis, prompted by the realization that my morning routine just wasn't giving me joy. I quit my job and traveled the world for 5 years, trying to explore this issue. After much soul-searching and meditative reflection, I realized that for me, the best morning experience comes from not having to make too many choices. So I started this service to help you eliminate some stressful choices from your life, too.

Hi! I'd prefer to remain anonymous if you don't mind. I helped Fritz start this business because he really has no business savvy whatsoever. I'm proud to be a part of what I feel is a really important endeavor. Life is so complicated nowadays, full of so many decisions. Why add the decision of what to eat for breakfast? Let that one come to you, am I right? Personally, my favorite breakfast foods are the kimchi omelette, scrambled eggs and tomato sauce, and Cocoa Krispies.
